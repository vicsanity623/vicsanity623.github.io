Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 9, 2026

1. Introduction

We respect your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use the Audio Stem Separator tool.

2. Data Processing (The Audio Files)

This service allows you to upload audio files for stem separation. Please be aware of the following:

  • Transmission: Your audio files are transmitted via an encrypted connection (HTTPS) to our processing server.
  • Processing: The separation is performed using the Demucs AI neural network.
  • No Permanent Storage: We do not permanently store, share, or resell your uploaded audio or the separated stems. Files are processed in temporary storage and are automatically deleted shortly after your download is generated.
